Audience

Signal

What every viewer leaves when your film ends.

When a viewer finishes your film, they leave a Signal — a reading, not a rating. Three lines — Vision, Craft, Resonance — and the one word that stayed with them.

As those signals gather, they become yours to read: what reached people, what reached them less, and the words they kept returning to. Feedback meant to help the next film — the reading opens here once your first film is live.
