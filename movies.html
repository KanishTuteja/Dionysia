Why this page is quiet

Dionysia opens with short films and series. Not because the feature matters less — because it matters most.

A feature film is a year of someone's life. Before we ask a filmmaker to premiere one here, the platform has to be worth it: a real audience, real curation, a room that does the work justice. We're building that first — in the open, with the founding circle.

The feature is the anchor format. It arrives as the circle grows — and the filmmakers in the room now will be the first ones asked to bring it.
